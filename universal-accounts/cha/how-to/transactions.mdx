Universal Accounts simplify cross-chain interactions by abstracting away the complexity of bridging assets and managing different chain configurations. With a single function call, you can execute transactions that span multiple networks, whether you're transferring tokens, interacting with DeFi protocols, or more.

In this guide, you'll learn how to implement cross-chain transactions in your application using the Universal Accounts SDK.
